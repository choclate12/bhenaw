To Meri Pwari Bhenaa

Zindagi mein rishtay sirf khoon se nahi dil se bhi bante hain 💖Likin Kabhi kabhi zindagi mein kuch log bina koi shor machaye aate hain lekin apni ahmiyat chhor jaate hain tu unhi logon mein se hai Ap mere liye ek behn jaisi hai mo-boli sahi lekin asli rishtay se kam nahi🥺❤️aur Ap Ki madad na hamesha mujhe himmat di hai. Har mushkil waqt mein ap mere saath khari rahi bina kisi shikayat kww💫💖 ap ki izzat mere dil mein bohot gehri hai Dua karta hoon Allah hamesha ap ko khushiyan din sehat din, aur har kadam par asaniyan ata karin. Ap mere liye ek bht khaas insan hai aur ye baat kabhi nahi badlegi
